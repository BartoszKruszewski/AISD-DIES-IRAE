$T(n) = T(\sqrt{n}) + O(1)$

Załóżmy, że $n = 2^{2^k}$

$T(n) = T(\sqrt{2^{2^k}}) + O(1) = T(2^{2^{k - 1}}) + O(1) = T(2^{2^0}) + k * O(1) = T(2) + O(k) = O(k)$

Wiemy, że $n = 2^{2^k}$, więc $log_2{n} = 2^k$, więc $log_2{log_2{n}} = k$, więc $T(n) = O(log_2{log_2{n}})$
